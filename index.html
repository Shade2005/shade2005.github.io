Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dua Connect ("we", "us", or "our") handles your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

Our app requires access to your device's location (GPS coordinates) to provide its core features:

  • Prayer Times: To calculate accurate daily prayer timings based on your geographic position.
  • Qibla Direction: To determine the precise direction of the Kaaba relative to your current location.

How it is used:

  • This location data is processed locally on your device.
  • We do not store your location on our servers.
  • We do not track your movement history.
  • We do not sell or share your location data with third parties.

You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings, but this will disable the automatic prayer time and Qibla features.

1.2 User Settings

We store your preferences (e.g., calculation method, theme preference, muted notifications) locally on your device to enhance your user experience.

2. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that collect personally identifiable information.

3. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on external servers, your data remains secure on your device.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
